# Design Guide: Concept 1 - The Institutional Standard

This document outlines the visual identity, color palette, and design principles used for **Concept 1: The Institutional Standard** (Otti One).

---

## 1. Visual Philosophy
- **Vibe**: Safe, government-grade, trustworthy, and precise.
- **Style**: Institutional Swiss Corporate (Evolution of Swisscom style).
- **Core Elements**: Strong grids, bold typography, clear hierarchy, and thick borders.

---

---

## 3. Typography & Styling

### Headings
- **Font**: Sans-serif (Inter/system-ui)
- **Style**: Black/Extra-Bold, All-Caps, Tight Tracking (`tracking-tighter`).
- **Subheaders**: Bold All-Caps with wide tracking (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

### UI Components (Buttons & Cards)
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: Deep Teal background with a 4px bottom border in Copper Bronze.
  - Secondary: Outline with 2px Deep Teal border.
  - Shape: Sharp corners (0 radius) or very minimal rounding.
- **Cards**:
  - Background: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`).
  - Border: 2px Deep Teal with very low opacity (`10%`).
  - Hover: Border becomes full Deep Teal.

---

## 4. UI Elements & Assets
- **Icons**: Lucide-React (Stroke Width: 2)
- **Badge**: "Verified Swiss Standard" (Shield icon, Deep Teal background).
- **Grid**: Strict 12-column layout for desktop consistency.
